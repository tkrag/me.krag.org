http:\/\/www.holocaust-uddannelse.dk\/<\/a> (English: holocaust-education)<\/p>\n

where they present educational materials for studying the Holocaust and related items.<\/p>\n

A group known as “Dansk Selskab for Fri Historisk Forskning” (The Danish Society for Free Hirstorical Research), has set up a webpage at:<\/p>\n

http:\/\/www.holocaustuddannelse.dk\/<\/p>\n

This page not only claims that there was no holocaust, but does it using a URL and site design that is si close to the “Real thing” that it must be made to purposefully mislead…..<\/p>\n

Amateurish, ridiculous and plain stupid abuse of the Internet makes me sick….
\nBut that isn’t enough. These people have the chutzpah to put the Electronic Frontier Foundations<\/a>, Blue Ribbon<\/a> on their site.
\nA ribbon that is supposed to indicate: ” EFF’s Blue Ribbon Campaign Fights Internet Censorship & Protects Online Free Expression ”<\/p>\n

How is piggy-backing off of someones site, with the explicit purpose of confusing, in any way related to Free Expression?<\/p>\n
