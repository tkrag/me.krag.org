We (wire.less.dk) are in the process of arranging a grass-roots wireless weekend in Copnhagen in the first weekend of March. It’s part follow-up to a recent workshop in Berlin (BerLon<\/a>) and part wireless geekhood.<\/p>\n

The program is available at Copenhagen Interpolation<\/a><\/p>\n

There will be people there from various community networks in Berlin, London, Copenhagen and hopefully elsewhere. There’ll be an antenna-building workshop, a track onm wireless networks for the developing world, a section on mapping and visualisation, follow-up on what we call the PicoPeering<\/a> agreement and hopefully we’ll build a wireless, pedal-powered bicycle over the weekend.<\/p>\n

Attendance is free and we might even be able to help with cheap accomodation. It’s a true grass-roots event, shaped by the people who participate, and whatever else happens, it’ll be fun…. (I promise), so anyone who’s in the neighbourhood or otherwise wants a chance to meet some of Europe’s community network pioneers, this is the place to be.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
