Acacia sets out with the determination to:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • demonstrate how ICTs can enable communities to solve their development problems in ways that build firmly on local goals, cultures, strengths, and processes;<\/li>\n
  • build a body of knowledge capable of identifying the policies, technologies, approaches, and methodologies instrumental in promoting the affordable and effective use of ICTs by marginalized communities, such as women.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/blockquote>\n

    Now they also have a great on-line resource<\/a> full of research results from the myriad projects funded through Acacia.<\/p>\n

    One of the Acacia projects that, although it’s closing on it’s fourth year is still remarkably useful is the wireless toolbox<\/a>.
