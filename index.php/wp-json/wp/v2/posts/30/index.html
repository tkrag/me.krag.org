Just a really short update, because i couldn’t seem to fall asleep, i finished my book last night in a similar situation (Neal Stephensons: In the beginning was the command line…<\/i>), so i decided to install netscape 6.
\nthe fact is there still are a number of things i wish my browser on this linux machine did better, so i thought to myself, why not install the newest browser for this os?
\nand here it is, my first blogger entry, written on netscape 6 for linux.<\/p>\n

Som of you may think that netscape 6 for linux is a strange subject to fill my blog with, considering my usually slightly loftier subjects, and the fact that i just quit my job to piss off to ghana for 3 months.
\nand i haven’t even mentioned the holidays that are coming up, and all the family issues they bring up. oh, and there is the fact that the girl i really love is coming back to copenhagen next week, after 3 months in germany. <\/p>\n

all those things considered, filling a few of the late night hours of insomnia with thoughts and deeds pertaining to upgrading some obscure piece of software, in order to let me view and interact with web-pages a little more efficiently, may seem strange. well, the mind works in mysterious ways, and right now mine is maxing out….<\/p>\n

i think i better try that sleep thing one more time.
