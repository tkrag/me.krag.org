On the other hand, I’m assuming that some people who come here to read news about ICT for development, Open Source and Wireless systems must be equally frustrated by descriptions of vacations, family outings, various musings on the general unfairness of life etc.<\/p>\n

I refuse to keep up to date more than one primary website, and frankly all of the subjects are dear to me. That, esentially, is why this site is called multiplicity<\/a><\/b>.<\/p>\n

My solution to this little conundrum: Have a look at the sidebar and you’ll see a new section called filter<\/i>. Under this section is a list of all the categories on this weblog. Feel free to unselect any that are less than fascinating to you. And select “Remember this info?<\/i>” to set a cookie that keeps you settings for the next time you visit. <\/p>\n

To see a single category only, you can click on the category name instead, or on the (rss) link to get a rss feed. <\/p>\n
