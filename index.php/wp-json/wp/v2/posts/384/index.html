The Register: Agere designs wireless VoIP chipset<\/a><\/p>\n

Media attention is growing in the area of Voice over WiFi. Enabling Wireless PDA’s or dedicated phone handsets to communicate via standard Voice-over-IP, would allow really low-cost, mobile telephony in the office, the warehouse or the home. <\/p>\n

One of the more important recent announcements in this area is Agere’s announcement that it will launch an integrated chipset that combines VoIP and WiFi, in one low-cost chip.<\/p>\n

Agere’s wireless VoIP phone technology offers the promise of making low-cost, mobile phone calls over the Internet more widely available once the chips become available from September. <\/bq><\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
