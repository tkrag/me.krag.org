According to this Press Release, NEC and Motorola are joining forces to bring out products in this space where WiFi wireless networks and Voice-over-IP converge. <\/p>\n

Yet another confirmation that we’re not alone in supposing this is going to be a really interesting area.<\/p>\n

Unfortunately the Press release is pretty much without content. It says nothing (really, it doesn’t) about timing, or whether there are specific goals for this cooperation. Oh Well. <\/p>\n

NEC America Inc. (Irving, TX) and Motorola Inc.
