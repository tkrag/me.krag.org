According to this<\/a> story on Wi-Fi Networking News, Less Networks is a Austin, Texas based community wireless effort,.
\nIt’s a volunteer-based company that develops simple solutions that let caf\u00e9 owners give away wireless internet access, while providing a bit of control over spammers, abuse etc., i.e. simple free wireless with management. <\/p>\n

They also provide volunteers to help caf\u00e9 owners actually set up the networks. <\/p>\n

This is a pretty cool idea, and the name Less Networks, is pretty close to our own wire.less.dk (or less.dk for short). <\/p>\n

I like the idea of helping caf\u00e9 owners provide free wireless, as a way to spread the gospel to the community. Let’s not underestimate the value of showcases that visualize how much cooler free networking is, when compared to commercial hotspot services.<\/p>\n
