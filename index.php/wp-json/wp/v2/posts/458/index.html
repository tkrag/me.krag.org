In the past 3 days, i’ve had the fortune to hear 2 extremey eloquent presentations about “Freedom”, as in Free Software, Hardware, Networks and especially culture.<\/p>\n

Both of these gentlemen are incredibly eloquent, moving and extremely convincing. And both of them manage to express the importance of holding on to the freedoms we have, in the face of grave pressure from corporate interests. Unfortunately at a conference like this, they are preaching to the converted.<\/p>\n
