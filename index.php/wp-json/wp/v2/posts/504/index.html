I had the pleasure of being interviewed for NPR (National Public Radio) on monday. It’s for a regular show called News & Notes that targets African-American audiences in the US with news stories of relevance, and in this case it was a brief segment about African Internet connectivity, and how it lags behind the rest of the world. <\/p>\n

I’m not entirely satisfied with the result, but then i wasn’t expecting to be, as short concrete answers that make good sound bites for brief radio segments don’t always convey the complexity of the real world. <\/p>\n
