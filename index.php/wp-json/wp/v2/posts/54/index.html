20010207 My Birthday coming up…. Invitation<\/p>\n

To all of you avid followers out there:<\/p>\n

This Saturday (February 10th) is my birthday.
I will be 27 years old.<\/p>\n

For that reason I am throwing a little informal party at our geek home Geekhalla.
So, if you are in Accra on Saturday, please feel free to drop by Geekhalla some time after 5 or 6 p.m.<\/p>\n

There will be a bit of beer, and hopefully some kebab or other small snacks, as well as a birthday surprise. And once the beer is gone, we’ll go out somewhere in the neighbourhood.<\/p>\n

Directions to Geekhalla:<\/p>\n

Geekhalla is in Osu, next to the Visa Section of the U.S. Embassy.<\/p>\n

From Danquah circle, walk down Cantonments.
Turn right just after the Elf station.
Take the fist left, followed by the first right.
Geekhalla is the last building before the US. Embassy on the right hand side.
It has a white wall with a large Geekcorps<\/a> logo on it.<\/p>\n

If you get lost, ask for the U.S. Embassy Visa Section in Osu (most cab drivers know it), walk around the building until you find the white wall with the geekcorps name and logo on it.<\/p>\n
