20010211 Thanks to slashdot and other comments….<\/p>\n

A quick thank you to slashdot for accepting Jasons post<\/a> on saturday. The result was an incredible 16,000% increase in page-requests compared to saturday the week before. Now I can convince myself that all those people visited because of my birthday!!!<\/p>\n

Also thanks to ABC, for a very nice article<\/a> about our efforts. I love to see my name in the media \ud83d\ude42<\/p>\n

And thanks to everyone who helped me celebrate my birthday at geekhalla in Accra yesterday. It was a great birthday, but I am a little dissapointed that you all didn’t finish the beer I bought. Now I have to drink it all myself.<\/p>\n

Special thanks to Modestu, Hans, Ally, Tim, Jean-Luc and Ethan for the wonderful presents. (If I forgot anyone forgive me, the heat is turning my brain into mush!).<\/p>\n

Amongst the things I got:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • a lovely Strawberry Daiquiri consisting of canned Russian Vodka, Strawberry Tang and boiled water. (Ethan really knows how to make the most of available ingredients)<\/li>\n
  • A beautiful turqoise piece of Kente cloth with the woven inscription “Happy Birthday Thomas” (Thanks Modestu, that is really beautiful)<\/li>\n
  • A Mary, Mother of Jesus plastic nightlight. (Thanks Tim, forgive me for my lack of ecstatic enthusiasm \ud83d\ude42<\/li>\n
  • A glass of Nutella (Thanks Ally, nothing like a thick, creamy chocolate substance at 38 degrees Celsius)<\/li>\n
  • A gift-box of Ghanaian chocolate. (From my fellow countryman Hans, who saw the posting on slashdot, went to this very site, saw the invitation and popped by with a present)<\/li>\n
  • A Pocket guide to great xxx. (Thanks Jean-Luc, I’m sure that will come in handy when my lovely girlfriend hopefully visits me here)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    It was a great party, even though both the water and the electricity went of during the evening. Lots of people, Ghanaians as well as ex-pats, and lots of beer in many variations. Thanks to everyone…<\/p>\n
