New design means new beginning…..
\nI got rid of all the old stuff….
\nWell, in fact it’s still here<\/a>, but I decided it was too antiquated to feature prominently….
\nSo from now on, it’s only the stuff from blogger.<\/a><\/p>\n

My CV and stuff has moved to my company site, now that I’m an independent contractor in close liason with my good friend Jens.<\/p>\n

Our Company is called:
\nMostly<\/font>Harmless<\/font>
\nIn honour of Douglas Adams<\/a>, incredibly humourous authour of the Hitchihikers Guide Trilogy, as well as a few other books, and comedian writer extraordinaire….<\/p>\n

Well, in fact we also think the name is quite funny. I mean in a world where a huge number of IT projects continue to go over budget, over time, and end in minor catastrophies,
\nMostly<\/font>Harmless<\/font>
\nhas a certain irony. For now our corporate slogan is: “We Cause Less Damage…”<\/p>\n

Well, I hope you like the simplicity of the new design. For a tech guy it isn’t bad I think.
\nI’ll be back to talk more about what’s happened since I was last here…..<\/p>\n

Don’t forget to check out the site of my favourite NGO, and do volunteer for a trip to Africa if you’ve got the Tech skills. It comes highly recommended:<\/p>\n
